Chocolate Chip Cookies

Satisfy that sugar craving when you make easy cake mix chocolate chip cookies. With only 4 ingredients, you can enjoy ooey gooey chocolate in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow Cake mix
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine all the ingredients (except chocolate chips) in a bowl. The batter will be thick.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Spoon onto cookie sheets leaving about 2 inches between cookies.
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until they start to brown SLIGHTLY on the edges.
  6. Allow them to cool slightly before taking them off the cookie sheet. Finish cooling on a cookie rack.
